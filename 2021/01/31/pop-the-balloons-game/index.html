Android game:Pop the Balloons

Author: Mengxiao Li Date: January 31, 2021  20:23:19 Category: Tech-Projects

Description:

This is an Android game.
Game starts with a random-chosen requirement of target balloons’ color and shape.
Players need to pop the right balloons, one point for popping one right balloon.
Balloons start at the bottom of the screen and move up at different speeds.
When time’s up, there is a pop-up screen showing the total score the player gets.
And the top 10 players will be listed in the High Score List.
